Brian Penney

With over 20 years  of experience in the tree business. 

Born in Ontario, Brian began his tree work in British Columbia, Canada in 1989.

By 1992 he had begun to run his own crews doing planting and spacing work.

In 1994 he had his own company specialising in tree planting and spacing. 

Between 1989 and 2001 he was responsible for the planting of over 10 million trees.

Working as a Ski Coach & Instructor in the Winter seasons he met an Australian girl and moved to Sydney in 2002.

Working both in Canada and Australia as a tree climber Brian studied in Vancouver and Sydney to gain qualifications for both countries.

In 2007 Brian opened Penney Wise Tree Services specialising in all aspects of tree care.

Brian now holds the following qualifications:-

  • Australian Certificate 3 in Arboriculture
  • Level 1 Rope Access
  • Senior First Aid
  • Industrial First Aid
  • Canadian Climbing Certification
  • Member of Australian Arborist Association

Our company is fully insured including Public Liability up to 20 million dollars.
