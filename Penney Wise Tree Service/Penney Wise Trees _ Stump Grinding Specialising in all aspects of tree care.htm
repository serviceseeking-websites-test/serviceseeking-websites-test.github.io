Stump Grinding

Email us to schedule an appointment.

After a tree has been cut down and all wood material is removed from site, a sizeable part of it remains firmly in place in the form of the stump and root system.

In most cases these stumps should be removed.  This may be due to the significant space they take up, the fact that they can be hazardous or they may interfere with planed changes to the property.

In order to remove the stump and surrounding roots, a machine called a "stump grinder" can be used to reduce the stump and root system into grindings.  This process is normally performed to a specific depth, usually 150-250ml below the surface.

If you are considering having stumps removed, it is best to consult a professional.  At Penney Wise Tree Sevices, we can incorporate stump grinding into any tree removal plan.
