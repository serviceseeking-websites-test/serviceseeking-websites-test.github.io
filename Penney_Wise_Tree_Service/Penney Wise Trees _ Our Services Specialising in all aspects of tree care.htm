EMERGENCY SERVICES

There are things in life that you can't control.  Damage to trees by storms is one of them.

At Penney Wise Tree Services, we are always ready to help during times of crisis.  If you or your property is in danger as a result of a tree failure, you can reach us day or night on 0438 796 997 and you will receive a priority tree service.

Our team has the experience and equipment to safely and efficiently resolve your emergency tree care needs so remember to add our number into your file of emergency contacts. 

CONSULTATION / PERMIT REQUIREMENTS

Each coucil has what is called a tree preservation order. This is essentially a document which spells out what tree works can, and cannot be undertaken without aproval from the council. Every coucil is different with regard to these rules. You will find these documents on every councils' web pages. By simply scheduling a consultation, we can provide guidance as to what is possible in your area.
