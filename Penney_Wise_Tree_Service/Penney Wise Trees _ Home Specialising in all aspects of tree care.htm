Penney Wise Tree Services offers professional tree care ranging from tree removals, tree pruning, chipping and mulching, stump grinding, land clearing, planting and tree reports.  Based in Sydney, we pride ourselves in our high quality tree work and exceptional customer service.

We are qualified Arborists, who hold comprehensive insurance and are committed to undertaking best practice OHS&E for all tree works we carry out.  We constantly strive to develop and maintain our high level of OHS&E practice and ensure this culture is instilled with all whom work for and alongside Penney Wise Tree Services.

Trees are often located in the most difficult of surroundings.  You can ensure that when we carry out your tree care works, surrounding infrastructure and safety measures are all carefully considered and incorporated in our tree works plan.  This tree works plan will be discussed with each client when we meet on site during our consultation process where all your needs and concerns will be addressed.

We also  provide a 24 hour emergency tree service to customers in the event of a tree emergency.  These customers will be provided with a priority tree service to ensure that risk to safety or property is restored immediately.
