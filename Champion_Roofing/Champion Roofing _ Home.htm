About Us

ROOF REPAIR EXPERT FOR OVER 20 YEARS

Champion Roofing is a roof repair specialist with over 20 years of experience in the industry. We have a "Fix It Once, Pay for It Once or It's Free the Second Time Around" policy for all roofing jobs.

Although our speciality lies in roofing, we also offer several other services including Security Services, and many other repairs services. We have a 100% satisfaction rating from customers across all our trades.

We offer Weekly Payment Plans for those in urgent need and no home insurance. We also offer insurance assistance for those with declined insurance (roofing services only).

No matter what you need, we'll do our best to help you out. Just give us a call and we'll talk about your job.
