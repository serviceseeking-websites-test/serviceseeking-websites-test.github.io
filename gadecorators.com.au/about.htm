ABOUT US

Paint. Decorate. Repair.

We paint, decorate, and repair homes!

We are a Brisbane-based painting and decorating business with over 22 years of trade experience. We're very competent in all fields within our trade and have licences, training, and experience to back us up.

Anything that needs a paint or decorative finish, repair, or plaster we can do! We have a passion in restoring deteriorating homes or offices and transforming them into something to be proud of.

We don't subcontract work - everything is done and completed by us! We cater to Brisbane, Gold Coast, and surrounding areas.
