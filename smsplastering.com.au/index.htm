ABOUT US

SMP Plumbing & Gas Services are advanced when it comes to plumbing maintenance & gas services offering great value, service and technology. We specialise in drain blockages and all household general maintenance and installations. We also offer solutions in emergency situations that require immediate attention 24 hours, 7 days a week. With 15 years’ experience and satisfaction guaranteed you will be sure to have peace of mind that your plumbing is in safe hands.

At SMP Plumbing & Gas Services, we believe that our customers’ needs are of highest importance and our team is fully committed to meeting those needs. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat clients and referrals.

At SMP Plumbing And Gas Services we operate an emergency service that is available 24 hours 7 days a week.

In cases of plumbing emergencies, our team are available 24/7 and will be at your home normally within 90 minutes.
