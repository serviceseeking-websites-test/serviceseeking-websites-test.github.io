What Hot Water System is for you?


Old School Plumbing and Gas serve both Commercial 

and Residential Hot Water System needs.


We pride ourselves on efficiency and will spend some time with you to find

out what you want and help you decide what Hot Water System is best for you.


Deciding what system is right for you can depend on many factors. The size

of your house ie. how many bedrooms you have, depending on the type and age

of your home, a Gas Continuous Flow Hot Water System may require a gas pipe

size upgrade.


Gas Hot Water Systems are More Cost Effective compared to Electric Hot

Water Systems. If your gas supply meter box is quite a distance from the

Hot Water unit a 5 star Hot Water System is the way to go as it uses less

energy to heat the same amount of water, costing you less.


A Continuous Flow Hot Water System heats your water as you need it, it uses

a supply and demand logic where as a Cylinder Hot Water System heats the

water within it and maintains the temperature constantly, so if you away

from home you are still paying to keep your water hot whilst you are not

home!


Electric storage units and electric continuous flow units are still used

but have become less favorable due to electricity price rises. Want to

upgrade to Gas Continuous Flow Hot Water? When gas is not available to your

home we can most definitely Supply and Install a new cylinder unit for you.


Old School Plumbing and Gas we recommend 

making it a 5 start unit to minimize cost.
