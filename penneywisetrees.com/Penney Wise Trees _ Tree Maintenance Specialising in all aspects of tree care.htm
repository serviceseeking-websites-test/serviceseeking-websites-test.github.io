Tree Maintenance 

At Penney Wise Tree Services we are trained in a variety of techniques and strategies to help maintain your trees. 

​These pruning techniques include:

  • Dead wood removal
  • Property overhang reduction
  • Assest protection
  • Remedial pruning
  • Crown thinning
  • Crown reduction
  • Crown cleaning
  • Removal of damaged, torn or snapped limbs

﻿Our trained arborists will provide you with the best advice  on how your trees will respond to pruning and will also advise on any council approvals that may be required to carry out requested work.

﻿All work is carried out in accordance to the Australian Standard AS 4373-2007 Pruning Amenity Trees.
