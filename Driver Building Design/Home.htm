WHAT WE DO

Driver Building Design is dedicated to providing you with a hassle free and personalised answer to your building drafting needs. Building drafting is the starting point for anybody who wants to build something as simple as an extension to an existing house, or something more complex such as a completely new house, office or business premises, and anything beyond.

Driver Building's particular specialty is listening carefully to your needs, large or small, and to meet those needs with professional and industry standard results. We guarantee you a timely, responsive and cost competitive service.

WHAT YOU'LL GET

Concept drawings to enable you to verify and review your design instructions. Quality industry standard architectural plans and details ready for engineering design and certification by the Building Certifier for submission to Council. Personalised and friendly service with a clear contractual agreement for time-lines and overall cost. A competitive hourly rate without large overheads.
