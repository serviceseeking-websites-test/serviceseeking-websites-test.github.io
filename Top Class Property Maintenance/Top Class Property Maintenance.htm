ABOUT US

Professional lawn cut provides the best possible finish for your lawn  including professional edging of entire lawn area including around trees and other garden features. 

Your local Top Class Property Maintenance can assist with your lawn care through weeding, top dressing. Ride on mowing is available for all properties and slashing is also available for larger properties. 

A well maintained garden provides a great look and feel to any
home and increases the appeal of your property. At Top Class Property
Maintenance we can have a maintenance program to suit your garden needs or simply provide a once off visit. 

We do all the work and then undertake a thorough clean up at the completion of the work to ensure that you regain the use of your garden and yard. We will take care of both hard and green waste and this service is an extremely cost effective solution for your clean up needs.
